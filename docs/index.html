Getting started

Begin with one honest week.

Download Brali

Install the app from the official store listing that matches your device.

Go to download options

Set a focus

Choose one area to improve. You can start from a Growth Zone, a task you have been avoiding, or a simple outcome you want to notice this week.

Create a daily planning rhythm

Add the next action to your task list or plan a Focus session. Make the scope small enough that you can complete it without needing a perfect system.

Check in and review

Use a check-in, journal entry, or metric to record what changed. At the end of the week, keep the practice, reshape it, or choose a different focus.

Keep a copy of your data

In the app’s settings, use backup, restore, or export tools when you want a copy of your local record.
