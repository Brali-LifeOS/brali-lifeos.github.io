Features

Everything in Brali serves the next useful action.

Explore

Growth Zones & library

Organize your focus across life areas, find practical ideas, save favorites, and create personal entries.

Plan

Tasks, events & focus

Make the work visible with tasks, planning tools, Focus sessions, and challenges.

Reflect

Check-ins, journals & logs

Capture what is happening with daily check-ins, journal entries, time logs, and review signals.

Learn

Key metrics & insights

Track the measures that matter to you, then use the record to spot changes over time.

Connect

Protocols & chains

Link useful practices together so a single intention can become a repeatable routine.

Keep control

Backup & export

Brali includes settings for local data, backup, restore, and export—useful when you want a copy of your record.
