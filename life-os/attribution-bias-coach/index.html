Understand Context in Behavior

When judging behavior: - For others: Ask, "What situations might have influenced their actions?" - For yourself: Reflect, "How much of this is about my choices versus circumstances?" - Balance the view: Consider both personality and context for all parties. Example: A colleague misses a deadline—consider if external factors, not just laziness, played a role.
