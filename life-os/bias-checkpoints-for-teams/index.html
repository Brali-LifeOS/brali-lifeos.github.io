When Interacting or Making Decisions: - Pause and Reflect:

Spot and Challenge Hidden Biases

When interacting or making decisions: - Pause and reflect: "Am I acting on assumptions based on someone’s background or appearance?" - Seek diverse input: Surround yourself with perspectives different from your own. - Use structured decision-making: Avoid snap judgments by following a clear process. Example: Hiring? Focus on qualifications and skills, not personal characteristics.
