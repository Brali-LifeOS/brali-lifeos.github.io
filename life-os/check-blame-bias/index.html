When Assigning Blame: - Pause: Ask,

Check Blame Bias

When assigning blame: - Pause: Ask, "Am I blaming more because the outcome was worse?" - Separate outcome from intent: Focus on actions, not just consequences. - Avoid personal filters: Check if you’re influenced by similarities to the victim. Example: In a car accident case, avoid assigning more blame just because the damage was severe.
