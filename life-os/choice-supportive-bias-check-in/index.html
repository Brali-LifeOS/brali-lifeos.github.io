After Making a Decision: - Be Honest: Reflect on Whether Your Choice Truly Delivered

Spot Choice-Supportive Bias

After making a decision: - Be honest: Reflect on whether your choice truly delivered the results you hoped for. - Compare alternatives: Consider how other options might have played out. - Learn for next time: Focus on improving future decisions rather than justifying past ones. Example: If you bought a gadget and it didn’t meet expectations, admit it instead of defending the purchase.
