Growth Zone

Ericksonian

Growth hacks based on Ericksonian Therapy, using stories and suggestions for change.

7 practical entries

  • Find a Quiet Place, Close Your Eyes, and Focus on Your BreathingFind a quiet place, close your eyes, and focus on your breathing. Let each breath relax you a bit more, guiding yourself into a calm, focused state.
  • In a Relaxed State, Press Your Thumb and Index Finger Together While Imagining aIn a relaxed state, press your thumb and index finger together while imagining a calm scene. Use this ‘anchor’ whenever you need calm.
  • In a Relaxed State, Take a Negative Thought and Imagine It from a DifferentIn a relaxed state, take a negative thought and imagine it from a different perspective. What positive lesson or insight could it bring?
  • Think of a Metaphor That Represents Your Goal or Challenge (eThink of a metaphor that represents your goal or challenge (e.g., ‘climbing a mountain’). Let this image guide and motivate you.
  • Think of a Time When You Felt Strong, Capable, or SuccessfulThink of a time when you felt strong, capable, or successful. Tap into that memory in a relaxed state to remind yourself of your inner resources.
  • While Deeply Relaxed, Visualize Yourself Achieving Your Goal, Feeling Every Detail of That SuccessWhile deeply relaxed, visualize yourself achieving your goal, feeling every detail of that success. Imagine it vividly, as if it’s happening now.
  • While in a Relaxed State, Repeat Positive Suggestions to Yourself (eWhile in a relaxed state, repeat positive suggestions to yourself (e.g., ‘I am confident,’ ‘I handle challenges calmly’). Imagine these becoming true.
