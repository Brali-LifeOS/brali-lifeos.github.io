When Reflecting on Past Events: - List the Facts: Write Down Both Positive and

Spot Euphoric Recall

When reflecting on past events: - List the facts: Write down both positive and negative aspects of the memory. - Get perspective: Talk to others who shared the experience to balance your view. - Be realistic: Remind yourself that every memory has highs and lows. Example: If you recall an old job fondly, note the challenges you faced there to keep things balanced.
