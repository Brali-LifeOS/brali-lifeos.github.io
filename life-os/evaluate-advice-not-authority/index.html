When Considering an Opinion or Advice: - Ask Yourself:

Question Authority

When considering an opinion or advice: - Ask yourself: "Am I agreeing because of the person or because of the evidence?" - Check the facts: Verify the information independently, regardless of who said it. - Value expertise over titles: Focus on the actual knowledge or experience behind the opinion. Example: A senior manager gives advice—analyze the content, not just their title.
