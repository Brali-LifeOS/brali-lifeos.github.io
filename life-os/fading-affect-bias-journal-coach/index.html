Balance the Fading Affect Bias

To stay balanced: - Acknowledge both sides: Write about unpleasant memories alongside the positive ones. - Reflect intentionally: Spend time thinking about what you learned from difficult moments. - Focus on growth: Use past challenges as a reminder of your resilience. Example: When recalling a failed project, consider the lessons learned rather than just forgetting the negatives.
