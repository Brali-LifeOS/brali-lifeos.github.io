Gestalt

Use Gestalt-inspired hacks to stay present and aware, resolving inner conflicts.

8 practical entries

  • Imagine Different Parts of You (like the ‘inner Critic’ or ‘playful Self’) Having aImagine different parts of you (like the ‘Inner Critic’ or ‘Playful Self’) having a chat. What do they have to say? Let them express themselves without judgment.
  • Imagine Having a Conversation with Someone Important in Your Life (past or Present), TellingImagine having a conversation with someone important in your life (past or present), telling them what you feel and think, or ask them for advice.
  • Notice Where in Your Body You Feel Stress, Joy, or Any Other EmotionNotice where in your body you feel stress, joy, or any other emotion. Try to relax or breathe into those areas and see what comes up.
  • Pause, Take a Deep Breath, and Ask Yourself, ‘what Am I Feeling Right NowPause, take a deep breath, and ask yourself, ‘What am I feeling right now?’ Focus on being fully present and aware of yourself in this exact moment.
  • Think of Any Old Grudges, Unresolved Issues, or Unsaid ThingsThink of any old grudges, unresolved issues, or unsaid things. Take a step towards resolving one, even if it’s just writing it down to process.
  • Try Doing Something Differently Than You Usually WouldTry doing something differently than you usually would. For example, if you’re usually quiet in groups, speak up. Notice how it feels to step outside your norm.
