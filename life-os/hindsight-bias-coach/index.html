Challenge Hindsight Bias

When reviewing past events: - List surprises: Note things that were unexpected at the time. - Avoid ‘should haves’: Focus on decisions made with the information you had then. - Compare predictions: Look back at what you expected versus what happened. Example: After a project, review what you thought would happen and compare it to the outcome.
