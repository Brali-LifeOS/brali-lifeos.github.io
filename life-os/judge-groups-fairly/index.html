When Judging Groups: - Challenge Assumptions: Ask,

See the Individual in the Group

When judging groups: - Challenge assumptions: Ask, "Does this apply to all members, or just one person?" - Seek evidence: Look for facts about the group, not just individual actions. - Avoid overgeneralizing: Treat group decisions as collective, not necessarily representative of everyone. Example: If one team member misses a deadline, don’t assume the entire team is unreliable.
