Positive Psychotherapy

Boost positivity with hacks to focus on strengths and good experiences.

7 practical entries

  • Do a Small Act of Kindness, Whether It’s Helping a Friend, Volunteering, or JustDo a small act of kindness, whether it’s helping a friend, volunteering, or just offering a compliment. Notice how it makes you feel.
  • Recall a Positive Experience from the Past WeekRecall a positive experience from the past week. Write down what made it meaningful and how it impacted you.
  • Spend Some Time Listing Your Personal StrengthsSpend some time listing your personal strengths. Think of qualities, skills, or talents that make you unique and capable.
  • Take Time to Identify the Values That Are Most Important to You, Like Honesty,Take time to identify the values that are most important to you, like honesty, creativity, or family. Write them down as a reminder.
  • Think About What Truly Matters to You and Set a Small Goal Aligned withThink about what truly matters to you and set a small goal aligned with your values. It should be something meaningful and achievable.
  • When You Face a Setback, Treat Yourself with KindnessWhen you face a setback, treat yourself with kindness. Remind yourself that everyone makes mistakes and that learning is part of growth.
  • Write Down Three Things You’re Grateful for Each Day, Whether Big or SmallWrite down three things you’re grateful for each day, whether big or small. Reflect on how these bring positivity into your life.
