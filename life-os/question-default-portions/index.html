Rethink Default Quantities

When consuming (e.g., food, resources): - Ask yourself: "Do I really need this much, or am I just following the default?" - Adjust portions: Take only what’s appropriate for your needs, not the suggested amount. - Set your own limits: Avoid letting external standards dictate your consumption. Example: Served a large plate of food? Eat until you’re satisfied, not until the plate is empty.
