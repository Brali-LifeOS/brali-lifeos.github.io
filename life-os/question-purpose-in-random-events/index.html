When Noticing a Purpose in Random Events: - Ask Yourself:

Question Purposeful Explanations

When noticing a purpose in random events: - Ask yourself: "Is there any actual evidence of intention or design here?" - Consider randomness: Some things happen by chance without any goal behind them. - Separate cause from purpose: Look for causal explanations rather than assuming intent. Example: A storm ruined plans? Don’t assume it “happened for a reason”—explore natural causes instead.
