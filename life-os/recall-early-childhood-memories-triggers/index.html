Explore Childhood Amnesia

To recall early memories: - Use triggers: Look at photos or listen to music from your early years. - Ask family: Talk to relatives about events you may have forgotten. - Write it down: Jot down fragments of memories as they surface. Example: A photo of a birthday party might help you recall more about the event.
