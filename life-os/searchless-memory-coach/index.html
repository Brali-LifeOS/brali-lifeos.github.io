Combat the Google Effect

To reduce dependency on search engines: - Write things down: Jot key facts in a notebook or app. - Memorize essentials: Focus on learning critical knowledge by heart. - Quiz yourself: Test what you know without looking it up. Example: Instead of Googling a frequent recipe, memorize it and practice making it.
