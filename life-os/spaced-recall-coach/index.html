Boost Retention: - Spread It Out: Review Material in Smaller Sessions over Days Instead

Use the Lag Effect for Learning

To boost retention: - Spread it out: Review material in smaller sessions over days instead of cramming. - Schedule reviews: Create a spaced repetition plan. - Take breaks: Let your brain consolidate information. Example: Study vocabulary for 20 minutes each day instead of 2 hours in one sitting.
