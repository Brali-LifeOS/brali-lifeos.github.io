Spot Unintentional Plagiarism

When you think you’ve had a novel idea: - Pause and reflect: Ask, “Have I encountered this idea before?” - Check sources: Revisit books, articles, or conversations you’ve recently engaged with. - Document origins: Note down where your ideas come from to avoid confusion later. Example: If you’re writing a report, double-check to ensure your “new” concept isn’t paraphrased from another source.
