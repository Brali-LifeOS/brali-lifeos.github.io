Avoid Source Confusion: - Keep a Log: Note Where You Heard or Read Key

Track Information Sources

To avoid source confusion: - Keep a log: Note where you heard or read key ideas. - Cross-reference: Before sharing information, double-check its accuracy and origin. - Ask yourself: “Am I mixing this up with another memory or story?” Example: Before presenting a new concept, confirm whether it’s from your research or a colleague’s suggestion.
