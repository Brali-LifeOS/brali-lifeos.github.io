Privacy

Your personal record should remain personal.

Brali LifeOS is designed around local storage: personal entries and progress are stored on your device. The app also provides tools for backup, restore, and export.

What this site collects

This static website does not use a login, a contact form, third-party analytics scripts, or advertising trackers.

What the app stores

Brali stores your personal app data locally. Depending on the features you use, that may include plans, check-ins, journals, logs, preferences, and saved library items.

Backups and exports

Use the app’s Privacy & Data settings to create a backup, restore a backup, or export supported data. Keep exported data in a place you trust.

Diagnostics

The app includes Firebase services for analytics and crash reporting. Its in-app privacy information describes performance and diagnostic data as separate from your personal local entries.

Questions

For privacy questions, email privacy@brainlighter.app.
