Support

Help for the part you are working on.

Before you write

  • Check that your app is updated through the store where you installed it.
  • For a problem with local data, do not uninstall the app before checking whether a backup or export is available.
  • Include your device type, app version, what you expected, and the exact steps that led to the issue.

Privacy or data question

Email privacy@brainlighter.app for questions about personal data and privacy.

Product help

Use the contact option in the app’s settings where available. This website does not collect support requests or require an account.
